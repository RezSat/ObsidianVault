# Tal - Ruy Lopez - Bishop Sacrifice on h7

## Position
White attacks kingside, black king castled.

## Idea
Sacrifice bishop on h7 to open king.

## When it works
- Knight can jump to g5
- Queen can join attack
- King has weak pawn shield

## Why it works
Forces king into open + tempo attack.

## Game reference
Tal vs NN, 1965

## My understanding
1. Sacrifice is not random
2. Follow-up is forced
3. Opponent cannot defend fast
